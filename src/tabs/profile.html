Apple Health import
Import readings your Apple Watch recorded. On your iPhone: Health app → tap your photo → Export All Health Data → save the zip, unzip it, and pick the export.xml inside. The file can be large (hundreds of MB+); it's read on your device and never uploaded — only the latest value of each metric is kept, and the raw file is discarded after reading.
