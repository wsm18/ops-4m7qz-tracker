Paths

grow the tree · keep it alive
This is your tree of growth. It branches into Paths — the great domains of a soldier and a person (Body, Mind, War, Craft, Command, Knowledge, Vitality, Self). Each Path carries skills, and skills carry sub-skills. Every skill has levels that build on each other — each level a concrete ability you prove with a promotion test. A skill can fade if neglected, shedding levels like a branch sheds leaves — but it never dies: it floors at Level 1, always ready to grow back faster than it was first earned. Tend the whole tree, or specialize a single bough toward its peak.
