OPERATIONS — ROTC Mission Tracker · Install Guide
=================================================
A military-themed life tracker built around your ROTC progression.
Installs on Windows, Samsung, and iPhone with its own icon — no app store,
no fees. Data is saved privately on each device.

WHAT'S INSIDE
-------------
• RANK & POSITION (you control it): your MS year, rank, and leadership role
  are a badge you edit yourself (starts at "MS2 Cadet / No leadership role").
  Update it when you pick up S1 or any other position. XP does NOT change it.
• FOUR SKILL TRACKS: Fitness, Tactics, Knowledge, Discipline — each levels
  up independently from the tasks you complete.
• MISSIONS (one-off tasks) & DAILY ORDERS (recurring habits), pre-loaded with
  meditation, training, quiz study, deep work, and more.
• AFT SCORECARD: input your 5 event scores after each test. It flags your
  weakest event, tells you which training sessions to prioritize, and tracks
  your score history.
• QUIZ TAB: 7 officer/enlisted knowledge banks (ranks & insignia, troop
  leading procedures, general knowledge, land nav, weapons, customs &
  courtesies, leadership). Pass one (>=80%) to earn merit points + Knowledge
  XP; it then becomes a daily review order so it sticks. Passing all of them
  secures the Knowledge objective.
• WORKOUT LOG (Log tab): after each session, pick which workout you did,
  enter duration, and log sets — reps×weight for lifts, hold time for planks,
  distance+time for runs. It auto-tracks your best per exercise and flags
  when something is improving vs. slipping, so you can spot stalls (like a
  deadlift that isn't moving) before the next AFT.
• MOMENTUM SYSTEM: clearing all daily orders triggers a "Perfect Day" bonus
  (merit + XP that grows with your streak), with milestone celebrations at 3,
  7, 14, 30, 60, and 100 days. A readiness bar up top shows how close you are
  to a perfect day, and warns you when a streak is AT RISK so you push to keep
  it. Best Streak is tracked so breaking a run actually stings.
• QUIZZES (16 banks, sourced from YOUR advance sheets & TCs): Army Values, The US Army, Customs & Courtesies, Leadership, Army Profession, Land Nav & Maps, First Aid, Tactics/Battle Drills, OPORDs/Mission Command, Cyber Branch, Weapons (M4/M9-M17/M249), Warfighting Functions, Communication Process, American Military History, Know Your Branches, and Regulations & Policy. ~124 questions, each citing its source. VERIFY against the originals.
• BRANCH & BOARD PREP (Board tab): Cyber Branch (17-series) reference facts
  pulled from your branch orientation material, plus a Talent-Based Branching
  checklist (accessions file, résumé, branch interviews, OML inputs, BrADSO,
  TS/SCI clearance, etc.). Completing tasks earns Knowledge XP + merit.
• MONTHLY BASELINE (Log tab): at the start (now) and the start of each month,
  the app prompts you to run a max-effort baseline test on key movements
  (push-ups, plank, deadlift, pull-ups, squats, 2-mile, SDC). The ADAPTIVE
  TARGETS then blend two signals — your session-to-session trend AND your
  monthly baseline — so a single bad day won't trigger a false "hold," and a
  fresh baseline that shows you got stronger re-anchors your targets upward.
• ADAPTIVE TARGETS: the FM tab shows auto-calculated next-session targets for
  every exercise you log — it bumps the target when you're progressing and
  tells you to HOLD (and why) when you've stalled, so you train smart, not
  just hard.
• FM TAB: your lean, bodyweight-first AFT training plan.
• R&R: spend merit points on real downtime (reading chapters, gaming, rest).

  ** IMPORTANT: The quiz content and AFT scoring are STUDY AIDS I generated.
     Verify every quiz answer against your real ROTC materials (FMs, TC
     3-21.5, AR 600-25) and your cadre, and check AFT points on the official
     score chart, before trusting them for anything graded. **

EDIT / EXPAND THE QUIZZES
-------------------------
Open quizbank.js in any text editor. Each question is a simple block:
  {q:"question?", a:["opt0","opt1","opt2","opt3"], c:1, e:"explanation"}
  - c is the index (0-3) of the correct answer.
Copy a block to add a question; copy a topic section to add a whole new quiz.

INSTALL (all 3 devices) — free hosting, ~60 seconds
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag this entire folder onto the page (keep all files together —
   index.html, quizbank.js, sw.js, manifest.json, and the icons).
3. You get a link like https://your-name.netlify.app
4. Open that link on each device and install:

WINDOWS LAPTOP (Chrome/Edge): install icon in address bar, or menu ->
  "Install Operations".
SAMSUNG TABLET (Chrome/Samsung Internet): menu -> "Add to Home screen".
IPHONE (must use Safari): Share -> "Add to Home Screen" -> tap the icon.

NOTE: Because the app now uses multiple files, the "double-click index.html"
shortcut may block quizbank.js in some browsers. The Netlify link above is
the reliable way to run it. (Chrome users can also run it locally fine.)

MOVING PROGRESS BETWEEN DEVICES
-------------------------------
Data is per-device. Tap "export backup" at the bottom on one device, then
"import backup" on another.

• THE WALL (Awards tab): your 'I Love Me' wall — add awards, ribbons, coins,
  badges/tabs, certs, milestones, and memorabilia; they accumulate as cards.
• THE WEIGHT (Weight tab): a READ-ONLY MIRROR of your standalone Weight app — your jars (Open/Standing/Kept/Dead), keystone, memorial lantern, and ledger, shown here but not edited here. Binding/keeping/breaking still happen in the real Weight app (where the rituals live). Tap 'Open the Weight app' to jump there; export your ledger from it and tap 'Update mirror' to refresh this view. Operations is the portal; the Weight app stays the source of truth. The link is pre-set to your hosted Weight app. A daily 'good morning' nudge reminds you to refresh the mirror with one tap when it's a new day (the app can't auto-read your OneDrive on a schedule — that needs a login + server a static offline app can't do — so the nudge is the private, free way to stay current).

• CLOUD FILE SYNC (footer: 'link cloud file'): on laptop/tablet (Chrome/Edge),
  tap it and pick or create an operations-data.json inside your OneDrive folder.
  The app then auto-saves your data to that file on every change, and OneDrive
  syncs it across your linked devices. Open the app on another linked device and
  it reads the latest. NOTE: this does NOT work on iPhone/iPad (Safari lacks the
  needed file API) — on iPhone keep using export/import. The browser may ask you
  to re-grant file permission periodically (a security feature). Last-write-wins
  if two devices save at once.
